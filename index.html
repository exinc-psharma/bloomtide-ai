About BloomTide

Three Words.
Infinite Possibilities.

Bloom. Tide. AI. — These three words are the soul of our project. We didn't just use them as a theme — we made them the entire mechanic of the experience.

The Tide is your creative input — a slider that shapes the mood. The AI is the engine that reads your word and the tide to generate something never seen before. The Bloom is the result — a living, procedural flower that exists only because of your unique combination.

How It Works

Powered by Creativity & Code

Every bloom is generated using procedural algorithms — no two outputs are ever the same.

🌊

Tide-Driven Design

The tide slider controls the mood — low tide creates delicate cool-toned petals, high tide unleashes bold, tropical blooms.

🧬

Procedural Generation

A seeded random algorithm creates unique petal shapes, colors, stems, and centers — deterministic yet infinite.

🤖

AI Seed Engine

Your word is hashed into a mathematical seed that the AI uses to determine every visual property of the bloom.

🎨

Canvas Rendering

Each bloom is drawn in real-time on HTML Canvas using bezier curves, gradients, and layered petal systems.

💾

Backend Storage

Your blooms are saved to our server. The community gallery grows as more people contribute their unique creations.

📱

Fully Responsive

From desktop to mobile — the generator, gallery, and every section adapts beautifully to any screen size.
